PRIVACY POLICY

Our Commitment

GraBeat and its developers are committed to protecting your privacy. The application does not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising frameworks, and does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any personal data.

Camera access is used exclusively for real-time hand movement tracking during gameplay and photo capture for the optional Photo Booth feature. All image data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Beyond the information Apple provides to developers by default, no additional data is gathered or retained.
